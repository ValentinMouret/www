Dioxydes

I woke up one day with the very nerdy idea of having a «map» (hashmap) of my public data always available, from a webpage or a CLI. At first, it was to have easy access to things I don’t want to remember, like the exact link to my Medium or Linkedin, or the tax ID of my company.

Then, the idea grew to be the place people would put their public data to help them regain power over what they share with the world (and companies).

If the idea sounds interesting to you, you can create an account right away!
